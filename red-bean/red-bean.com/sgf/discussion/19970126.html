Here is my response to Arno's call for a thorough going-over of the SGF spec: My only comment is that I think labeling BR, WR, HA, and KM as Go specific properties is incorrect. These are all concepts that make perfect sense in other games. It is the _values_ of these properties that should be defined in a game-specific fashion, not the properties themselves. Matt
