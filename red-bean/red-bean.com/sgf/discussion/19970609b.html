Date: Mon, 9 Jun 1997 13:52:21 -0400 From: Dave Dyer Subject: Re: SGF complaints One point about using SGF to represent internal game trees; If you do, you are free to use additional properties, or us the approved properties in unapproved ways, provided they remain internal and are not exported when the game tree is published.
