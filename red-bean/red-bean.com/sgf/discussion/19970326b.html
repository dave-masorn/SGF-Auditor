Date: Wed, 26 Mar 1997 19:02:46 -0800 From: Robert Jasiek Subject: Redundant Properties A few properties do not have much value. Maybe I am wrong and they are widely used, but I think that TB, TC, TW are redundant and might be computer-go properties. The proper information can be stored in RE and C. The three properties are not useful at all for area rules. Even with territory rules they can only be applied at the game end. Otherwise, there is the V property. Also ON is redundant and GC and C (at a move node) can be used. -- Robert Jasiek jasiek@berlin.snafu.de
