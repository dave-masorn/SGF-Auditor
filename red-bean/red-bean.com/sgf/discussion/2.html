Date: Tue, 18 Jun 1996 18:17:26 +0200 (MET DST) From: Arno Hollosi Hi all, thanks to all who took the time to have a look at the draft. I updated the draft as requested: * dropped line limit * changed specifiaction for formatted text * '\' in SimpleText/Text escapes any char following it * no restriction of mixing verbose / short mode * changed verbose move-type (uses uppercase letters now and may be extended to hold positions up to 625x625 * SZ[]-value is defined as (number | number ':' number) now * format for DT, RE is mandatory * ISO-latin-1 isn't used in SimpleText/Text (but everywhere else) * ST[] updated -- (lowercase markup added) Just have a look at http://www.cis.tu-graz.ac.at/home/hollosi/sgf4_draft/ (I included a short index of properties now) Open questions: --------------- * Should we extent the Text format? (I'm thinking about choice of 3 fonts (or faces of the same font: no, light and heavy emphasis (e.g. standard, italics, bold face)) * What do you think about the new properties? escpecially AP, XS, ST? * Is the new move-type ok? (if boards are < 26) it's humanreadble. Ok for >= 26x26? * the draft tries to fix the time-properties (especially byo-yomi). Does the draft succeed in fixing the problems? * should we include other new properties, e.g. LA (LAnguage), CA (ChArset in Text/SimpleText) * Should we move the seldom used markup (BM, TE, UC, DM, ...) out of the standard and move it into an extended set (XS with positive number, i.e. general accepted extension to FF[4]) * Should we include directed graphs in the standard, or should we keep this an extended set? * What about computer go properties? I don't know anything about them myself. They should be an accapted XS[], but does anyone have a complete list with description? * Printing: SGF is not very useful, if someone wants to print a record. FG[] alone isn't enough. SGB uses DG[] (some kind of Diagram-spec), but the format is not public. /Arno
